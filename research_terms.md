## Critical Mistranslations to Avoid

### 生産技術 (Seisan Gijutsu)
- **INCORRECT**: "manufacturing technology"
- **CORRECT**: "Production Engineering"
- **Note**: 生産技術 is PE (equipment planning, process design), NOT general manufacturing

### 生産準備 (Seisan Junbi)
- **INCORRECT**: "production preparation" (generic)
- **CORRECT**: "Production Preparation (24→0 month PE workflow)"
- **Note**: Specific Toyota process, not just generic prep

### 治具 (Jigu)
- **INCORRECT**: "tool"
- **CORRECT**: "jig, fixture"
- **Note**: Positioning and holding devices, NOT cutting tools

### 自働化 vs 自動化
- **自働化 (Jidouka)**: Jidoka - Automation with human touch (TPS principle)
- **自動化 (Jidouka)**: Regular automation
- **Note**: Different kanji, different meanings

### 工程図 (Koutei-zu)
- **INCORRECT**: "process diagram" (generic)
- **CORRECT**: "Operation Drawing, Process Drawing"
- **Note**: Shows operations, fixtures, inspection points - NOT design drawing

## Usage Notes

### For J-STAGE Searches
- Use Japanese keywords for best results (J-STAGE indexes Japanese content)
- Combine track-specific terms with "Toyota" to narrow results
- Search by journal code for targeted results (JSPE, JSAE, etc.)

### For Patent Searches (J-PlatPat)
- Use applicant names in Japanese (株式会社ジェイテクト, etc.)
- Combine with IPC classifications for precision
- Focus on B21, B23, B24 for Track 2 (PE) patents

### For General Research
- Track 2 (PE) terms are most critical - highest knowledge gap
- Watch for mistranslations (生産技術 ≠ "manufacturing technology")
- Supplier names are key for finding technical papers
